# Welcome to the Tonatiuh++ Development Blog

This blog documents the ongoing development and enhancement of Tonatiuh++, an advanced solar ray tracing software for analyzing the optical behaviour of solar concentrators.

<!--truncate-->

## About Tonatiuh++

Tonatiuh++ is an enhanced version of the Tonatiuh ray tracer with improvements in ray tracing algorithms, performance, and cross-platform support.

The source code can be found [here](https://github.com/CST-Modelling-Tools/tonatiuhpp).

The wiki providing information regarding capabilities and usage can be found [here](https://github.com/CST-Modelling-Tools/tonatiuhpp/wiki)
